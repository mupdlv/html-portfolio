The Best Movies According to Dylan

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Tucker & Dale vs. Evil (2010)

Alan Tudyk and Tyler Labine bring the house down in this riotous flick! They put a dynamite twist on those beloved horror clich√©s. Every mix-up and mishap gets its moment in the spotlight. This movie takes a wild, entertaining romp through the world of horror with a fresh, fantastic approach. With sharp humor and a cast that'll leave you in stitches, it's an absolute blast!

Terrifier (2018)

Terrifier is a blood-soaked rollercoaster of terror, with a wickedly twisted sense of humor! David Howard Thornton's Art the Clown is a menacing marvel, carving a path to horror greatness. He's the enigmatic, unhinged charmer who'll give you the creeps with his theatrical malevolence and relentless mind games. The gore, the kills, and the brutality are cranked up to eleven. Sure, there are some jaw-droppingly outrageous moments, but remember, this film thrives on not taking itself too seriously, so why should you? The acting may occasionally tiptoe into cringe territory, but it's all part of the delightful B-movie homage. Don't expect an intricate plot; it's Art being eerily sinister, dispatching startled victims left and right. If you're a horror fanatic, this is a must-watch! Get ready to be both terrified and tickled.

I Spit on Your Grave (2010)

This flick is not for the faint-hearted, let me tell ya! The first half dishes out a jaw-dropping, heart-pounding scene that'll make your hair stand on end, especially if you're a lady. But hang in there, because the second half is like a satisfying dessert after a wild ride! Watch as the baddies face the music, each punishment tailored to their own misdeeds and words. This movie is an unforgettable, pulse-pounding adventure you won't want to miss!
